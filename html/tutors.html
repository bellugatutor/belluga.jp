Frequently asked questions

Q. How can I list my services on WyzAnt.com?

A. Tutors on WyzAnt.com come from a variety of backgrounds and include certified teachers, industry professionals, graduate and undergraduate students and more. To get started, simply complete an online application. A WyzAnt staff member will review your application and respond to you within 24-48 hours.

Q. How much does it cost to be listed on WyzAnt.com?

A. Listing your services on WyzAnt.com is free and unlike other marketplaces, WyzAnt does not accept payment for premium listings or top placement in the search results. WyzAnt's marketplace is fair and unbiased, making it easy for students to find the right tutor based on their individual needs.

Q. Am I eligible to be a tutor on WyzAnt.com?

A. If you’re passionate about helping students excel academically, we encourage you to complete an application. In order for your application to be considered, you must be at least 18 years old, reside in the United States, and possess a valid Social Security Number. Tutors are not required to be certified teachers.

Q. How does WyzAnt help me get new students?

A. Millions of people visit WyzAnt.com every month in search of private tutors. Having a profile on WyzAnt makes it easy for students and parents to find and contact you through a trusted, secure marketplace. Additionally, WyzAnt will notify you of new tutoring jobs in your area every day!

Q. How do I get paid?

A. WyzAnt processes all payments, offering students discounts and flexible payment options while ensuring you receive payment for your time. After a completed lesson, simply report your time. WyzAnt will collect payment from your student, deduct WyzAnt's service fee, and issue your payment by direct deposit or check. Learn more about WyzAnt's service fees.

Q. Is this in-person or online tutoring?

A. Both! You will be able to offer students in-person as well as online tutoring.
