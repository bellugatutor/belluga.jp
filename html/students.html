Frequently asked questions

Q. What is WyzAnt?

A. WyzAnt.com was started in 2005 with one purpose in mind: to make it as easy as possible for students to find their perfect tutor. Tutoring is a very personal experience and it's important that students find a good fit in a tutor to get the most out of each lesson. With over 73,100 qualified tutors, WyzAnt makes it easy for students to find that perfect tutor.

Q. What kind of credentials do the tutors have?

A. Tutors on WyzAnt come from a variety of backgrounds and include certified teachers, industry professionals, graduate and undergraduate students and more. All tutors have been reviewed by WyzAnt staff and passed subject proficiency exams in the subjects that they teach. Many tutors feature verified background checks and you may order an up-to-date background check on any tutor.

Q. How much do lessons cost?

A. Tutors are able to set their own hourly rate, typically between $30-$50/hr. You are not required to make a payment until after a lesson takes place and you are never required to commit to a minimum number of lessons. Similar to making a reservation at a hotel, we do require you to add a form of payment prior to beginning lessons so that we may bill you after your lesson takes place.

Q. How do I pay for lessons?

A. Payment can be made by credit card or PayPal after lessons take place or by check if paid in advance. After a lesson occurs, your tutor will report the time along with a detailed lesson review. WyzAnt will process payment and send your receipt via email. WyzAnt also offers discounts of up to 15% for prepaid package purchases.

Q. Where does the tutoring take place?

A. You and your tutor are free to choose any convenient location for lessons. Many students choose to have lessons in their homes, while others prefer to meet in a public location such as a library or coffee shop. Some tutors on WyzAnt also offer online tutoring for additional help in between lessons or in lieu of in-person lessons.

Q. Does WyzAnt offer any guarantee?

A. WyzAnt offers a 100% Good Fit Guarantee on the first hour of lessons with any tutor. WyzAnt will cover the cost of your first hour spent with any tutor if you are not completely satisfied. We understand that the right tutor must not only be qualified, but also capable of connecting with a student's unique personality and learning style.
